What is ImmuneTrail

ImmuneTrail is an AI-powered health education platform focused on helping individuals and families better understand immune health through insights based on published medical research.

Why ImmuneTrail

Today, we are facing a growing rise in chronic conditions that are often complex and difficult to fully understand. ImmuneTrail was created to help organize and simplify this complexity by providing educational, research-based insights across clinical screening, nutrition, and physical wellness.

ImmuneTrail App

ImmuneTrail App is an educational AI clinical screening tool designed to help families identify immune-related red flags based on published medical research.

The app provides structured screening pathways for immune dysregulation and PANS/PANDAS-related symptom patterns.

ImmuneTrail does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
