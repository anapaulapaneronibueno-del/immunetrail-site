# ImmuneTrail

## AI for Immune Health & PANS/PANDAS Screening

ImmuneTrail is an educational AI clinical screening app designed to help families identify immune-related red flags based on the latest published medical research.

The app supports structured educational screening pathways for general immune dysregulation and PANS/PANDAS-related symptom patterns.

ImmuneTrail does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. All content is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical care.
