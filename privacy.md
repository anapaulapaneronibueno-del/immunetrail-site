# Privacy Policy – ImmuneTrail

ImmuneTrail respects your privacy.

We do not sell or share personal data. Any information entered in the app is stored locally on your device unless you choose to create an account and use cloud-based features.

If you create an account, your email and basic account information may be securely stored using trusted third-party services.

We use secure technologies to protect your data and do not collect unnecessary personal information.

ImmuneTrail is designed for educational use only and does not collect sensitive medical records beyond what you choose to enter.
